## Quick Start: Top 5 Must-Know Slang (2024)

1. **Rizz** - Charisma game (from Chicago drill music)
  *"He's got that natural rizz with the ladies"*


2. **Glizzy** - Hot dog (DC/Maryland origin)
  *"Grab me a glizzy at the game"*


3. **Bussin'** - Extremely good (Southern Black vernacular)
  *"This gumbo is bussin'"*


4. **Cap** - Lie (Atlanta trap influence)
  *"No cap, I saw Drake at Walmart"*


5. **Ick** - Turn-off (TikTok viral)
  *"He vaped and gave me the ick"*
